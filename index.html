Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

OyoSays collects information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, city, bio, and profile photo when you create or update your account. We also collect content you post, comments you make, and interactions such as likes and follows.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve OyoSays
  • To personalise your feed and show you relevant content from Oyo State
  • To send notifications about activity on your posts and profile
  • To enforce our community guidelines and terms of service

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your public profile, posts, and comments are visible to other OyoSays users. Your email address is never shown publicly.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Profile photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account information at any time
  • Block users you do not wish to interact with
  • Report content or users that violate community guidelines
  • Request complete account deletion by contacting our admin

6. Cookies & Local Storage

OyoSays uses browser session storage only to maintain your login state. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

7. Community Guidelines

OyoSays is a platform for the Oyo community. Users must not post hateful, abusive, or false content. Violations may result in account suspension or permanent ban.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns or account deletion requests, contact us at [email protected].
